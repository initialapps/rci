Ruen Come In,

is a group of beautiful contemporary Thai houses, which were designed to reflect Chiang Mai rich culture and its craftmanship. The main building is made of Golden Teak Wood, which is highly regarded as an invaluable wood for its beauty and durability properties.

Rooms

Ruen Ngam Grand Suite

Our original room in the teakwood house. This room provides 1 double bed. This spacious suite is 99 sq.m. It is decorated with celadon, teakwood furniture and other northern style handicrafts. Guests can relax by dipping in Jacuzzi with air pool and whirlpool water system, revitalizing themselves in Far Infrared Sauna cabin in spacious bathroom or rest at serene balcony.

Rosewood Deluxe Rooms

Rosewood Deluxe room is refine through the art of decomposing several old lanna houses in Chiang Mai's rural area and reassemble them into a beautifully designed architecture. It is a mixture of Teak wood and Rosewood. Guests can indulge themselves in whirlpool bathtub or relax in private balcony overlooking our luscious garden.

Ruen Come In Restaurant

Set in a Northern-Thai-Style Teak Wood House, Ruen Come In Restaurant serves your appetite with Thai and Northern Thai food along with local fruit juices.
